About Us

Fostering Entrepreneurial Excellence: IEDC YCET's Journey Since 2014

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) cell started functioning in our campus from 27th January 2014. IEDC YCET is a dedicated initiative that fosters an entrepreneurial mindset among students and supports them in transforming innovative ideas into successful ventures. It serves as a platform for students in the form of various kinds of programs, workshops, mentoring sessions, hackathons, and startup competitions that helps the students to explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and nurture an entrepreneurial spirit.

Due to the boundless dedication and hard work of the members of IEDC YCET, we were awarded the TOP PERFORMER of South Zone Region as part of the Innovators Premier League(IPL) 2021-2022.
